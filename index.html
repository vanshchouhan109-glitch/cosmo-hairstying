Meet the Cosmo Team

Your Premium Hair Care Experts

Welcome to Cosmo Hairstyling — a luxurious and spacious salon located in the heart of Rotterdam Weena. Our highly hospitable team of expert stylists is dedicated to providing you with the ultimate salon experience.

Whether you need a quick after-work haircut, a dramatic color transformation, or specialized curl recovery, our team (Ranko, Pieta, Vanity, Robin, Diana, Stacy, and Yvonne) will deliver perfect results with meticulous attention to detail.

About Cosmo Hairstyling

Meet the Cosmo Team

Your Premium Hair Care Experts

Welcome to Cosmo Hairstyling, a premium, spacious, and luxurious hair salon in the heart of Rotterdam Weena. Our highly hospitable team of expert stylists is dedicated to ensuring you leave feeling confident and beautiful.

Our philosophy is simple: a hair appointment should never be rushed and should always be a luxurious experience. Whether you're visiting for a quick after-work haircut, complex color correction, or specialized curl recovery, our experienced team—including Ranko, Pieta, Vanity, Robin, Diana, Stacy, and Yvonne—is here to provide unmatched personal attention.

From the moment you walk in and are handed an excellent cappuccino, you'll know you're in the right hands. Premium hair products, expert technique, and genuine care. That is the Cosmo Hairstyling promise.
